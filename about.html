Our Mission

Give students more ways to move around campus—safely, clearly, and on‑demand. If it helps classmates get around easier, we’re in.

⚙️

How It Works

  • Sign in on the map page.
  • Pick a green marker and tap “Rent Now.”
  • Ride. The timer runs while you do.
  • Tap “End Ride” where you stop—the app calculates your cost.
💸

Transparent Pricing

Bikes & Snow‑bikes: $0.50/min. E‑bikes & Scooters: $1.00/min. Charges are rounded up to whole minutes—no hidden fees.

🛠️

Built at a Hackathon

A lightweight Flask backend with a Google Maps front end. In‑memory data keeps it simple for demos—restart to reset.
